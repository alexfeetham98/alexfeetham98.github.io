About Me

Hi! I’m Alex, a games programmer based in Bristol and I’ve been making games for about 7 years. I have recently graduated from the University of the West of England Bristol (UWE Bristol) having achieved a merit in MSc Commercial Games Development. During my studies I have developed strong programming skills with experience in the Unity Game Engine and C# while utilising GitHub for source control and collaboration.

I've had an interest in computers from a very young age and my attention was first drawn to video games in early 2010 when my dad took me into work with him at Microsoft HQ in Redmond, WA. That day was the internal unveiling of the Kinect for Xbox 360. I remember 11 year old me sitting in the auditorium watching the demonstration thinking it was the coolest thing ever! The day got even better as we ended up coming home with our own Kinect as we’d been selected or the beta program. This thoroughly ignited my passion for video games and I’ve been playing ever since. When I came to towards the end of my schooling I deciding what I wanted to do for the rest of my life there was only one clear option, I wanted to make video games. This drove me to choosing computer science, maths and games development at college to set me up to get into UWE to earn a masters degree in commercial game development.

Below are some of the projects I’ve had the opportunity to work on as a part of my studies as well as some my extracurricular activities.
